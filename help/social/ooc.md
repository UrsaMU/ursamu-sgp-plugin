OOC

Send an out-of-character message to everyone in your current room.
The message is prefixed with a themed OOC tag.

SYNTAX
  ooc <message>     Speak out of character.
  ooc :<pose>       Pose out of character.

EXAMPLES
  ooc Sorry, AFK for a sec.    <OOC> Alice says, "Sorry, AFK for a sec."
  ooc :waves.                  <OOC> Alice waves.

SEE ALSO: +ooctag
