+I

See what another player is carrying. (+inv is an alias.)

SYNTAX
  +i   <player>
  +inv <player>

EXAMPLES
  +i Alice          List Alice's visible items.
  +inv Bob          Same.

NOTES
  Same-room targets are visible to everyone; staff can inspect any
  target and see opaque/dark items.

  For your own inventory, use 'inventory' (no plus).
